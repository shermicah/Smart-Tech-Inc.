About Us

  • About

Smart Tech Inc. is a business venture developed by 4 individuals for the sole purpose of making the life of farmers and horticulturist easier. Smart Tech Inc. offers its first ever irrigation system, S.I.S, which is designed and developed by the company. Smart Tech Inc. operates as a General partnership business. Smart Irrigation System (S.I.S) developed by smart tech Inc. is an automated system which would replace the manual way or waters and caring for plants with the use of technology.


Revenue will be primarily generated through the sale of SIS. The goal is to reach $20,000 in the first year of sales and increase by 10% every year after. The main start-up capital will be generated using loans.


The entire project is spearheaded by Rhys Redhead who is the project manager and main programmer. The Designing of the physical system is done by Alden McGuilivary and Kaddim Beggs. Shermicah Mitchell Lewis is responsible for the Documentation and management of finance.
